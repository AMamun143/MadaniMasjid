Madani Masjid

Madani Masjid is a is a not-for-profit, religious and non-political organization established in 2004 and a Islamic house of worship in the Woodside, Jackson Heights, Elmhurst, Maspeth area.

Madani Masjid was established by some honorable brothers in the year 2004 in a rental space (71-05 Woodside ave) located in Woodside. Initially, the masjid started with 5 times daily prayers, Jumuah prayers, Taraweeh, Iftars and the Weekend Maktab program for students as well as yearly programs. As the population of Muslim community in Woodside increased, Madani Masjid was unable to accommodate everyone and was in need of their own space. In the year 2010, Madani Masjid was able to purchase their own place at 41-51 69street Woodside NY 11377, a former church and has been offering many services including : 5 day prayers, Jumuah prayers, Taraweeh, Eid prayers, Weekend Maktab School, Weekly Darsul Quran programs, funeral services, community events and many more.

With the vision of establishing an Islamic School and an Islamic Center as well as providing other services including a community hall, library, Madani Masjid has purchased the next door building for $1.2 million.
